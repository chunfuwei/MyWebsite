About Our College

The College of Physical Education is dedicated to excellence in sports education and athletic development

Our Mission

The College of Physical Education at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications is a secondary teaching unit directly under the university. Our primary responsibilities include delivering physical education to all students, developing high-level sports teams, organizing mass sports activities, and advancing sports discipline construction.

Faculty & Resources

Our college currently has over 60 faculty and staff members, with professors and associate professors accounting for more than 40% of the total. Our team includes international/national referees and national-level coaches. We are equipped with modern sports facilities including a comprehensive gymnasium, swimming pool, and track and field stadium.

Academic Programs

We offer more than 20 public physical education courses such as basketball, volleyball, martial arts, and swimming. All undergraduate students are required to complete the "Physical Education and Health" course. Our curriculum is designed to promote lifelong fitness habits and holistic student development.

Our Facilities

World-class sports facilities for training and competition

Comprehensive Indoor Stadium

Our multi-purpose indoor facility hosts basketball, volleyball, badminton, and gymnastics competitions with seating for 3,000 spectators.

Olympic-Size Swimming Pool

50-meter competition pool with 8 lanes, diving platforms, and modern water filtration systems for year-round training.

Track & Field Complex

International-standard 400m synthetic track with field event areas for javelin, discus, long jump, and pole vault.
