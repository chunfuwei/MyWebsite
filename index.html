About Our College

The School of Physical Education at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT) is committed to cultivating top-notch professionals in physical education, social sports guidance, and martial arts training.

We offer undergraduate and graduate programs that emphasize scientific training, teaching excellence, and social responsibility. Our students are encouraged to engage in diverse campus activities, competitions, and research to build a strong foundation for future careers.

Academic Achievements

Our college has made outstanding contributions in the fields of sports science, physical education research, and athlete development. Faculty members actively publish research papers, lead national-level projects, and mentor students in innovation competitions and academic forums.

Students have won numerous awards in national university sports competitions, and the school has built collaborations with international institutions to promote academic exchange and research visibility.
